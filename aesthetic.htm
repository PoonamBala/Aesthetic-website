My little heaven on earth,
But you wouldn't move an inch,
you'd never have to
I'd run to the rim of the world to catch you,
My eternal peace of mind,
Right before the dawn could get to you just - in time
I'd sing the songs in the lullabies
I'd read the myths of the ancient tribes
Just so I would know the prophecy
That you are somewhere in the galaxy,
After the sun sets, above the clouds, before the night comes and - or maybe behind the moon and all that we know to be true
I knew, I'd see you because
There was this stardust on the grand canyon
When I was young it made me feel a little hazy
But to find you my love, somewhere in this brand new world, I'd have to be somewhat crazy

My heart just might be whole as a traveler, but it's you that I miss.
